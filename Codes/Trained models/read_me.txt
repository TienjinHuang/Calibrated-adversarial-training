Naming rule: 
e.g. RN_b_5_b1_01_e8_cw.pth
RN: Preact ResNet18
b_5: the hyperparameter belta is set to 5
b1_01: the hyperparameter belta_{1} is set to 0.1
e8: the epsilon is set to 8 
cw: the model is trained by CAT_{cw}
